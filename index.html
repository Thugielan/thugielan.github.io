About SweetBites Bakery

SweetBites Bakery is a small local bakery that provides fresh cakes, cupcakes, cookies, pastries and beverages. Our goal is to give customers affordable, delicious and beautifully presented baked products.

This website is designed to help customers view our menu, check prices, explore our gallery and contact us easily. Our target customers include students, families, office workers and dessert lovers.
